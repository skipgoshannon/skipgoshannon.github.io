Studio Friend Pass Policy & Liability Waiver

Ceramics • Printmaking • Darkroom

The Friend Pass allows active Tradeshop members of Waldo’s and Company to bring a guest (“Friend”) into one of our creative studio spaces for a single-day visit. Please read the following terms and waiver carefully.

Friend Pass Overview

Studio

Daily Rate

Inclusions

Ceramics

Access to wheels, handbuilding tools, supervised use of studio space. Any projects left behind will be the responsibility of the member to trim, glaze, etc. 5lbs. Of clay will available for $5.

Access to non-toxic tools (presses, brayers, rollers); bring or purchase paper/ink.

Access to B&W darkroom and enlargers; bring your own paper/negatives/film and chemicals.

Terms & Conditions

  • Friend Passes are available only to active members of Waldo’s and Company.
  • Passes are valid for one calendar day and are non-transferable.
  • A tradeshop member may bring no more than two Friends per day using this pass.
  • Tradeshop member is responsible for insuring friend engages in all safety procedures and clean up.
  • Tradeshop member must make sure Friend is using only the clay that is available at Waldo’s and Company.
  • Friends must be actively supervised by the sponsoring member at all times.
  • Friends must adhere to all studio rules, safety protocols, and staff directions.
  • Studio staff may revoke access at any time if behavior is unsafe or inappropriate.
  • Friends are not permitted to operate kilns, mix chemicals, or use hazardous materials.
